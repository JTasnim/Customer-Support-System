Tuition and Costs















Affordability and transparency are central to our mission, which is why we charge $31,000 less than the average U.S. private school, according to the U.S. News and World Report’s review of 2023-2024 tuition rates. As you prepare to embark on the next chapter of your educational journey, take a moment to review the total cost of attendance. 









Undergraduate Cost of Attendance


Tuition and fees are charged on a semester basis. The regular undergraduate tuition rate is $330 per unit. Additional fees cover registration, campus and learning resource expenses for each semester.
Estimated Yearly Tuition and Costs (based on two semesters) for the Bachelor Programs





Tuition


$7,920




Fees


$910




Textbooks Costs


$1,200




Health Insurance Premium


$1,040




Estimated Total Charges


$11,070










Notes:

This estimate includes tuition, fees, textbook costs, and a health insurance premium, which are subject to change. All students are required to pay current rates for tuition and fees each semester. 
The cost of textbooks is estimated to be approximately $150 per course. The actual cost of textbooks can vary significantly from course to course.
All students must purchase their own textbooks/materials.
All students are required to have an adequate health insurance plan. Students can purchase health insurance through SFBU or an outside vendor.
Refund Policy
Health insurance and textbook/materials considerations may possibly reduce the costs noted above. For more information or questions, please contact Student Services.
SFBU students residing in University Housing also incur housing costs: $1,000 per month/$4,000 per semester.














Graduate Cost of Attendance
Tuition and fees are charged on a semester basis. The graduate tuition rate is $450 per unit. Additional fees cover registration, campus and learning resource expenses for each semester.

The cost of textbooks is estimated to be approximately $150 per course. The actual cost of textbooks can vary significantly from course to course.
All students must purchase their own textbooks/materials.
All students are required to have an adequate health insurance plan. Students have the option to purchase health insurance through SFBU or an outside vendor.
Refund Policy
