Financial Literacy 101: Empower Your Financial Future!






        October 31, 2024 | 12:00pm - 12:45pm
              







 





Financial Literacy 101: Empower Your Financial FutureJoin us for an engaging and practical workshop on financial literacy! Learn essential skills like budgeting, responsible borrowing, managing debt, and understanding your financial health. We’ll also introduce an interactive scholarship tool to help you navigate education costs without relying heavily on loans. Whether you’re just starting your financial journey or looking to refine your money management, this session will equip you with the knowledge to make informed decisions and set yourself up for success.Don't miss out—take control of your financial future today!Perks: Pizza and soft drinks provided, open to all undergraduate and graduate students
Note: This is the same workshop as the one on October 8th.
