Undergraduate International Application Workshop

Are you an international student ready to apply to San Francisco Bay University? Join our International Student Application Workshop for step-by-step guidance through the application process. Learn how to navigate admissions requirements, visa procedures, and scholarship opportunities, all while getting tips on how to make your application stand out.
Our experienced admissions team will be on hand to answer your questions and provide the support you need to confidently submit your application and plan your journey to SFBU.
