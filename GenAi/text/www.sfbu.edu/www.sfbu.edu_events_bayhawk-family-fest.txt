Bayhawk Family Fest






        October 25, 2024 | 5:30pm
                  - October 26, 2024 | 2:00pm
              







 





Get ready for a fantastic time at our inaugural Bayhawk Family Fest! Join us on October 25th at 5:30 PM for an evening reception with SFBU's leadership team. We promise this event to be a blast for the whole family. Located at 161 Mission Falls Ln in Fremont, our gathering will be packed with exciting activities, delicious food, and great company. Whether you're looking to enjoy some festive games, indulge in tasty treats, or simply relax and soak in the autumn vibes, there's something for everyone.
​Continue the fun on Saturday October 26th with a day full of workshops and activities.
​We will accommodate the first 50 students to register and two guests. Deadline is Friday October 4th, 2024. UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS ONLY.
​We hope to see you there!
