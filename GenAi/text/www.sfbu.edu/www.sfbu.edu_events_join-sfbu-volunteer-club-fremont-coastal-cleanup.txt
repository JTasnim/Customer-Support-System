Join the SFBU Volunteer Club for Fremont Coastal Cleanup






        September 21, 2024 | 9:00am - 12:00pm
              







 





The SFBU Volunteer Club will be back in action on Saturday, Sept. 21, as part of the Fremont Coastal Cleanup Day. The event will take place in Fremont's Central Park from 9 a.m. to noon. Meet in front of Aqua Adventure Park. Let's show the local community what a great community we are at SFBU with participation from students, staff and faculty.
Volunteers should:
Wear long pantsWear solid shoes/sneakers (no flip-flops or sandals)Wear glovesWear an SFBU shirt if you have oneBring waterArrive in front of Aqua Adventure Park by 8:45 a.m.If interested, please contact michael.leinhos@sfbu.edu or call/text 408-348-1947.
